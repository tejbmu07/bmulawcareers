Recruitment Process

01
Browse the Batch
Explore all 17 candidate profiles. Filter by specialisation, CGPA, or credentials. Each profile includes internship pedigree, achievements, and a personal statement.
02
Contact Placement Cell
Email placement@bmu.edu.in with your firm's name, roles you are hiring for, and a shortlist of candidates. We respond within 24 hours.
03
Schedule Interviews
We coordinate with candidates to schedule interviews at your firm, on campus, or virtually. Panel interviews can be arranged for multiple candidates.
04
Make Offers
All offer communications are routed through the placement cell for transparency. Candidates are available from July 2026 unless otherwise noted.
